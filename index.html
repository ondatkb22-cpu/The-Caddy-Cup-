🤝 Zurich Classic — Team Format DFS

The only team event on the PGA Tour. 74 two-man teams compete over 4 rounds — alternate shot (R1, R3) and best ball (R2, R4). DraftKings uses 4-team lineups under a $50k salary cap. This completely changes the math vs. individual events.

🔄 Format Matters

Alternate shot rewards ball-striking consistency and partnership chemistry. Best ball rewards pure upside and ceiling. Your picks need to be good in BOTH formats over 4 rounds.

⚡ Value Signal

Positive Delta = DK underpriced this team vs. the betting market. In a team event, pricing mistakes are amplified because you need 4 teams under cap, not 6 individuals.

🚀 GPP Strategy

Best ball rounds are where GPP lineups are won or lost. Target teams with at least one elite ball-striker (SG:APP) and one elite putter (SG:Putt) — they complement in alternate shot and both go off in best ball.

📸 Key Edges

TPC Louisiana is a bombers track with soft conditions typical in April. Length + SG:APP off the tee matters more here than at Harbour Town. Teams with two long hitters are particularly valuable.

💡 Top Teams to Watch

✅ EARLY TARGETS

Clark / Moore (1/80) — Wyndham Clark won here in 2023 with teammate. Taylor Moore is a consistent ball-striker. Strong value potential.

Koepka / Lowry (3/80) — Elite chemistry. Both major champions. Koepka's length + Lowry's scrambling = elite alternate shot combo. Lowry won here with Pádraig Harrington.

Fitzpatrick / Fitzpatrick (2/80) — Brothers. Proven team chemistry. Matt is in top form post-RBC.

Griffin / Novak (12/80) — Ben Griffin in excellent form, Andrew Novak is a strong value pairing. Under-discussed.

⚠️ CAUTION

High ownership chalk — Clark/Moore will be massively owned. Their salary likely reflects this.

Untested partnerships — Teams playing together for the first time (many in the 40-74 range) have significant alternate shot risk. Chemistry in alt shot is real.

Players out of form — Both members need to be healthy and competitive. Check injury reports before lock.
