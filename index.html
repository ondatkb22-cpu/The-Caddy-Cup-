Course Profile

Distance dominates. Driving distance has a 0.993 correlation with total course fit at Doral. Accuracy adj is actually −0.517 negatively correlated with fit.

Bermuda greens. TifEagle, fast.

Water on 10 holes. Bogey avoidance matters; wayward drives get penalized hard.

Wind exposure. Open links-style, exposed fairways. Wind players get an edge.

🎯

How We Build

Distance fit first. Every cash anchor must be in the top 20 by driving_dist_adj. No exceptions at this course.

Tout buzz = anti-signal. High buzz + high ownership = CHALK = fade for GPP.

Form filter. Poor-form players (2+ cuts in last 5) get a −4 penalty on cash projection.

Long-course bonus. Players with 0.85+ SG over 20+ starts on courses 7,400+ yards get a +4 boost.

📊

What the Scores Mean

SG · 50/50 blend of recent form (SG L20) and baseline talent. Normalized 0-100.
Fit · DataGolf course fit at this course. Pure distance bias here.
Form · Last 5 events. good=0 cuts + 2 top10s. poor=2+ cuts.
Lev · DK pricing vs course fit + market. Positive = underpriced.
Tier · Buzz × ownership. SHARP = smart pivots. CHALK = fade. CONTRARIAN = no one talking.
Equity · Win + top-10 odds rolled into 1-5 stars.
💰

Cash vs GPP

Cash games (50/50, double-up). Need to beat field median (~452 DK pts). Maximize cash_proj. High-floor anchors. Ownership doesn't matter.

GPP (tournaments). Need top 20% (~497) or better. Maximize ceiling × leverage − ownership penalty. One bust kills you, one spike wins big.

Break-even cash: 55.6% on a $25 DU. Below that, you're burning EV.
